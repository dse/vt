HP 700/60 and 700/70 Display Terminals

Superb ergonomics, plus new windowing flexibility
 
 

Overview | Key Features | Choosing | More Info

 

Display terminals overview

HP 700/60 terminals have always offered the highest levels of user comfort, safety, productivity and economy. Now those benefits are multiplied...

The HP 700/70 Windowing Terminal is a high quality, mouse-driven windowing environment for fast, multi-session windowing to multiple UNIX hosts. Not only is it a complete solution, including host window management software, it runs existing UNIX applications unmodified.

A built in ROM card door on both models allows easy, cost-effective upgrades from the 700/60 ASCII text terminal to the 'windowing' 700/70 display terminal. It also provides built-in upgradability to future 700/70 upgrades.
 

Key Features

Both models are designed for maximum user comfort and productivity...

  • Low-glare, fast-refresh, tilt and swivel, 14" borderless screen.
  • Enhanced resolution 10 x 16 character cells.
  • Detachable, full-adjustable keyboard.
  • All models meet stringent safety and regulatory standards for emissions. 'ES' models go further, meeting Swedish MPR guidelines.

...and both offer easy connection and system management.

  • De-facto industry standard compatibility: Wyse 60 in both native and PC terminal modes; plus DEC VT320/220/100/52 personalities.
  • Two built-in serial ports (one RS-232C and one RS-232C/RS-422) and one Centronics parallel port.
  • Unrivalled reliability: MTBF rating of over 100,000 hours (over eleven years!).
  • Easy ROM card upgrades from 700/60 to 700/70, and to future 700/70 models.
 

How to choose between HP 700 models

HP 700/60 terminals are high quality ASCII text terminals. HP 700/70 'windowing' terminal add even greater levels of user productivity and comfort by allowing multi-session windowing.

Whilst all models are designed to meet high safety standards, 'ES' models meet the stringent Swedish MPR guidelines.
